Finding your ID

SteamHour needs your numeric Steam ID or your Vanity URL in order to work its magic. Due to how the Steam API operates, using your login or nickname won't work.

Vanity URL

The vanity URL is the simplest one:

  1. Go to your profile page.

  2. Click the Edit Profile button

  3. Look in the textbox that's marked "Custom URL".

Numeric SteamID

Finding your numeric ID is a little more complex. There are several ways; we believe this one is the simplest:

  1. Go to your profile page.

  2. Right-click the background and select "Copy Page URL"

  3. If you paste the URL into a text editor (like notepad), it will look a little bit like this:

    http://steamcommunity.com/profiles/00000000000000000/

  4. The list of numbers is your numeric Steam ID.
